About Me

11 Years in the Field.
Now Building for the Web.

My name is Okumu Jared — an Electrical Supervisor with over 11 years of hands-on field experience in power generation, HV/LV electrical systems, solar PV, and industrial infrastructure. I currently serve with UNMISS (United Nations Mission in South Sudan) in Torit State, where I lead a team of 14 technicians maintaining mission-critical power systems for UN operations.

I have personally commissioned fifteen 500 kVA diesel generators, designed and installed a 100 kW hybrid solar PV system, and supervised the full electrical infrastructure for facilities across South Sudan — generators, HV/LV distribution networks, air-conditioning, and cold rooms — all to international EHS and QA standards.

I am now combining that technical authority with web development to serve engineering firms, solar installers, generator companies, and NGOs who need professional websites. My advantage is simple: I speak your technical language, I understand your clients, and I build websites that reflect the credibility your business deserves.
